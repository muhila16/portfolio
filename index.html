About Me

I am currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Bioinformatics at VIT. I have a strong foundation in biology and an emerging expertise in technology, which I have seamlessly integrated to create innovative solutions. My projects include the development of a device using tinyML for reproductive wellness and a website to prevent child abuse through interactive games.

Academic Journey

Starting with no background in Computer Science, I quickly adapted and excelled, overcoming challenges in programming and mathematics. My interest in Web Development and early startup development led me to earn certifications in the MERN stack and build several impactful projects.

Achievements

I have received recognition for my innovative projects, including a MeitY Government Grant of Rs. 1 Lakh for my project MyCycle, which uses tinyML for menstrual cycle tracking and preterm delivery prediction. Additionally, I have won various competitions and awards, including the best paper award for my device and algorithm.

Projects

My projects focus on utilizing technology to address real-life problems. From developing a reproductive wellness device to creating a multi-language website for child abuse prevention, I aim to make a positive impact with my work.
